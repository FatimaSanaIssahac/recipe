Easy and Super Fudgy Brownie Recipe

If you're someone who finds the kitchen intimidating but has an insatiable sweet tooth, this super easy and fudgy brownie recipe is just for you.
Follow these simple steps to create a delicious treat that will leave everyone wanting more!

Ingredients:

  • 100g butter
  • 150g chocolate (dark or milk, as per your preference)
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla essence (optional)
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons cocoa powder
  • Chocolate chips or chunks (optional)

Instructions:

  • Step 1: Melt Butter and Chocolate
    1. In a heatproof bowl, melt the butter and chocolate together using a microwave or a double boiler.
    2. Stir the mixture until smooth and well combined. Set aside to cool slightly.
  • Step 2: Beat Eggs and Sugar
    1. In a separate mixing bowl, crack the eggs and add the sugar.
    2. Beat the mixture until it becomes creamy and slightly pale in color.
  • Step 3: Combine Mixtures
    1. Slowly pour the melted butter and chocolate mixture into the egg-sugar mixture.
    2. Add vanilla essence (if using) and mix until fully incorporated.
  • Step 4: Add Dry Ingredients
    1. Sift the flour and cocoa powder into the bowl with the wet ingredients.
    2. Gently fold the dry ingredients into the mixture using a spatula.
    3. For extra indulgence, stir in some chocolate chips or chunks.
  • Step 5: Bake the Brownie
    1. Preheat your oven to 200째C (392째F) for 15 minutes.
    2. Line a baking pan with parchment paper or grease it lightly with butter.
    3. Pour the brownie batter into the prepared pan and spread it evenly.
    4. Reduce the oven temperature to 170째C (338째F) and bake for 25 minutes.
    5. Check for doneness by inserting a toothpick or fork into the center. It should come out slightly moist for a fudgy texture.

  • Serving Suggestions:

    • Let the brownie cool slightly before slicing. Serve warm with a scoop of vanilla ice cream, or enjoy as it is with a cup of coffee or milk.

  • Storage Tips:

    • Store the brownies in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 3 days. For longer storage, refrigerate them for up to a week or freeze for up to a month.

Your super fudgy brownies are ready to be devoured! Perfect for any occasion or just to satisfy those late-night cravings.
